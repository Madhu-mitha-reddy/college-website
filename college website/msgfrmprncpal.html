Message From Principal : Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College

MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

Greetings. With great pleasure, I welcome you to Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College (SVEC), an Autonomous Institution, managed by Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust. SVEC is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, recognized by Government of Andhra Pradesh, and affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Ananthapur, Ananthapuramu.

Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College was established during the year 1996 to create, nurture and shape technical professionals and leaders to create an inclusive and sustainable society in a National and International perspective. To achieve this, SVEC is offering Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral programs that nurture many vibrant and promising professionals equipped with skills to face the ever changing social, economical and technical landscape of our country. I am very much pleased to share with you that SVEC is accredited by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) and also UG programs are accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation).

In SVEC, it is believed and practiced that excellence is a continuous process and in pursuit of which the institute has made deep forays into contributing renowned technocrats, successful entrepreneurs, competent leaders, innovative scientists and researchers.
